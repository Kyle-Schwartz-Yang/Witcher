Merchants of Ofir

They came from overseas lands shrouded in a veil of mystery. With them they brought wonderful goods that cause both delight and horror. Along the way, merchants from Ophir will meet all the factions of GWENT - some will want to trade with them, while others will want to steal goods or take them by force.

Black Sun

Some say that the world will perish in ice, others that it will burn in fire. Still others heed the ancient prophecies, foreshadowing the approach of eternal darkness. Once a century, the light of the sun fades, and the shadows envelop the world of the living - they destroy, corrupt, bring madness. But even in a world afflicted with curses and troubles, there are ancient places untouched by evil, and heroes that pave the way through the darkness.

Master Mirror

He has always been. He pulled the strings, weaved human destinies and decided how the most important events in the history of the continent would end. Even if you want to circle this trickster around your finger, in the end he will subordinate everyone and everything to his devilish will. He is Mr. Mirror, and how it all ends depends only on him.
